The Silent Echo

4K Sci-Fi • 2023 • 14m 15s •
star 4.8

In a dystopian future where silence is the only currency left, a young archivist discovers a hidden recording that could dismantle the oppressive regime. Hunted by the Soundkeepers, she must traverse the ruins of the old world to broadcast the message before her voice is stolen forever.

Winner of the Best Cinematography award at the 2023 Neo-Noir Film Festival. "The Silent Echo" explores themes of censorship, memory, and the enduring power of the human voice.
