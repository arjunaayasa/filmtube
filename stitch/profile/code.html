About the Creator

Sarah Jenkins is an award-winning independent filmmaker based in Portland, OR. Specializing in psychological horror and sci-fi thrillers, she combines practical effects with minimal VFX to create grounded, terrifying atmospheres.

Her breakout short, "The Silent Woods," premiered at Sundance in 2021. She is currently in pre-production for her first feature-length film, funded entirely by this community.
